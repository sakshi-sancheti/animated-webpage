Parallax Scrolling Effect

This webpage demonstrates parallax scrolling effect.
What does that mean?
Parallax scrolling is a web site trend where the background content (i.e. an image)
is moved at a different speed than the foreground content while scrolling.
This creates kind of animated effect on webpage.
